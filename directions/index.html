Directions

The IROS Agrirobotics Workshop 2025 will be held in Hangzhou, China, a city renowned for its beautiful West Lake, rich history, and vibrant culture. Hangzhou is easily accessible by air, train, and road, making it a convenient location for international and domestic attendees.

Venue

The conference will take place at the Hangzhou International Conference Center, located in the heart of the city. The venue is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and is surrounded by hotels, restaurants, and cultural attractions.

Getting to Hangzhou

By Air: Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport (HGH) is the main airport serving the city, with direct flights from major cities worldwide. From the airport, you can take a taxi, airport shuttle, or metro to reach the conference venue.

By Train: Hangzhou is well-connected by high-speed rail to cities like Shanghai, Beijing, and Guangzhou. Hangzhou East Railway Station is the primary hub for high-speed trains and is just a short metro ride from the conference venue.

By Road: Hangzhou has an extensive network of highways and expressways, making it easily accessible by car or bus from nearby cities.

Local Transportation

Hangzhou has an efficient public transportation system, including metro, buses, and taxis. The city also offers bike-sharing services and scenic boat rides on West Lake.

Accommodation

There are numerous hotels near the conference venue, ranging from budget-friendly options to luxury accommodations. We recommend booking early to secure your stay.
