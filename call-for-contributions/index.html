Call for Contributions

We invite researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to submit their contributions to the IROS Agrirobotics Workshop 2025. Topics of interest include but are not limited to:

  1. Robotic design for agricultural applications
  2. Control systems for plant-robot interaction
  3. Autonomous navigation in agricultural environments
  4. AI and machine learning in agricultural robotics
  5. Sustainable practices in agricultural robotics

Submission Guidelines

  • Workshop paper type: a) Extended abstracts (2–4 pages, novel research); b) 1-page summaries of accepted IROS papers.
  • Presentation format: a) Top papers: oral presentations; b) Others: poster sessions; c) Submissions may be rejected if criteria are unmet.
  • Publication: Accepted abstracts will be posted on the workshop website or archived in the WUR library (will not appear in the official IEEE proceedings).
  • Formatting: Submissions must follow the IEEE conference template (PDF only).
  • Submission: Upload contributions via the submission portal.
  • Peer review: a) Review by 2 referees; b) Authors must reciprocally review others’ papers.

Special Journal Issue

  • Selected papers will be invited to extend their work and submit to a special issue of Computers and Electronics in Agriculture (COMPAG).
