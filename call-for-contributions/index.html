Call for Contributions

  • We invite researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to submit their contributions to the IROS Agrirobotics Workshop 2025. Topics of interest include but are not limited to:
  • Topic areas:
       - Robotic design for agricultural applications
       - Control systems for plant-robot interaction
       - Autonomous navigation in agricultural environments
       - AI and machine learning in agricultural robotics
       - Sustainable practices in agricultural robotics

Submission Guidelines

  • Workshop paper type:
       a) Extended abstracts: 2–4 pages (novel research);
       b) 1-page summaries of accepted IROS papers.
  • Presentation format:
       a) Top papers: oral presentations;
       b) Others: poster sessions;
       c) Submissions may be rejected if criteria are unmet.
  • Publication:
       Accepted abstracts will be posted on the workshop website or archived in the WUR library (non-archival, no IEEE proceedings).
  • Formatting:
       Submissions must follow the IEEE conference template (PDF only).
  • Submission:
       Upload contributions via the submission portal.
  • Peer review:
       a) Review by 2 referees;
       b) Authors must reciprocally review others’ papers.

Special Journal Issue

  • Selected papers will be invited to extend their work and submit to a special issue of Computers and Electronics in Agriculture (COMPAG).

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: September 1, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: September 20, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Submission: October 10, 2025
