Welcome to IROS Agrirobotics 2025

Agricultural robotics is pivotal to addressing global food security, yet challenges persist in integrating design, control, and plant-robot interaction. This workshop bridges these domains, offering a platform for interdisciplinary dialogue. Innovations in soft robotic manipulation, adaptive control for unstructured environments, and plant-responsive sensing will be highlighted. Unlike prior workshops focusing on isolated themes, this event synthesizes cutting-edge advances across three pillars: design (e.g., biomimetic mechanisms), control (e.g., AI-driven decision-making), and plant-robot interaction (e.g., real-time phenotyping).

Technical relevance to IROS 2025 lies in its alignment with RAS Technical Committees (Agricultural Robotics, Bio-Robotics, and Automation) and Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 2: Zero Hunger).
